More than kungfu

We help our students be the best they can be, in and out of the dojo.

The word "Kaizen" means to make marginal improvements day by day. We help you develop physical, mental and emotional discipline to achieve mastery. Consistency develops confidence in mind and body.

About
Kaizen Martial Arts

Welcome to Kaizen Martial Arts Association!
Classical Kungfu, modern instruction.

We strive to teach classical kung fu and practice it the way it was done traditionally while also combining modern techniques to create well rounded martial artists. By training at our Martial Centre, students will develop their strength, speed and fitness while building up their confidence and learning how to defend themselves in case of a dangerous situations. The classes are great workouts for young and old and can be adjusted for any fitness and skill level.

Kids Lessons

Channel your child's energy into a fun-filled activity and get them out of the house engaging with teachers and students. Children of all ages welcome. Learning martial arts when young has wide-ranging outcomes for young learners like...

  • Improved Focus and learning ability
  • Improved discipline, consistency and emotional stability
  • Improved social interaction & Self-Confidence
  • Build a solid foundation of fitness, strength & flexibility
  • Learning co-ordination and body awareness
  • Kungfu training is excellent preparation for sports

Adults & Teens

  • Learn self-defence, perfect for young adults
